Questions

Every question
you might have.

Does it cost anything to become an agent?+
Nothing. Zero. RACE makes money from the platform fee that stations pay — agents never pay anything to join, operate, or withdraw. You are the growth engine, not the customer.
How long do I earn from each person I invite?+
Permanently. Once someone registers under your code, they're linked to your wallet for life. No expiry, no reset. As long as they fill up at any RACE station, you earn — even years later.
Can I invite companies and fleet accounts?+
Yes — and fleet accounts are the highest-value invitations you can make. A company with 20 vehicles filling 15 times per month can generate over 1,000,000+ IQD/month to your wallet from a single invitation.
What happens when new stations join RACE?+
Your existing invitees automatically earn at new stations the moment they go live — and so do you. Your network value grows with every new station, with zero effort from you.
When do free towing and roadside assistance kick in?+
Free towing unlocks when you reach 10 active invitees (Bronze Agent). Full roadside assistance unlocks at 30 invitees (Silver Agent). These are personal benefits for you — on top of everything your invitees receive.
Is there a limit to how many people I can invite?+
No limit. No cap. No ceiling. RACE scales with you — the more you build your network, the more you earn and the better your agent benefits. There is no point at which more invitations stop paying.
